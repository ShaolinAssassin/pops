h1. *IRX Loader*


______________________________________________________________________________________________________________


POPStarter has an IRX loader that lets you use your own external drivers (as IRX files). These IRX files must be named MODULE_#.IRX (from MODULE_0.IRX up to MODULE_9.IRX) and must be placed into your POPS folder. They will not be loaded from the VMC game folder.
