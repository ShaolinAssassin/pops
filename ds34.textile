h1. [DS3] *Use DualShock 3 Controller with POPStarter*


______________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Credits : "belek666":https://github.com/belek666/Open-PS2-Loader for the DualShock 3 modules.

*Notes :* 
. works only with WIP 06 betas, does *NOT* work with the prototypes (due to a bug affecting the IRX loader). 
. modules compiled and provided by belek666 - 2017/06/25 @"ASSEMblergames":https://assemblergames.com/threads/ps2-pops-stuff-popstarter.45347/page-106#post-939746.

* "DualShock 3 modules - USB mode":https://bitbucket.org/ShaolinAssassin/popstarter-documentation-stuff/downloads/DS3_modules_USB_20171017.7z
* "DualShock 3 modules - Bluetooth mode":https://bitbucket.org/ShaolinAssassin/popstarter-documentation-stuff/downloads/DS3_modules_BT_20171017.7z

These modules are ready to use - you just need to download and copy them into your POPS folder as they are.

pre. 
POPS/MODULE_1.IRX
POPS/MODULE_2.IRX

FYI : 
. MODULE_1.IRX = USBD.IRX 
. MODULE_2.IRX = usb_pademu.irx / bt_pademu.irx

You can switch from analog to digital mode by pressing !https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/4289708765-Playstation-Button-PS.png(title)! + !https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/2984521000-Playstation-Button-Select.png(title)! - with $D2LS in a "GAME/CHEATS.TXT":https://bitbucket.org/ShaolinAssassin/popstarter-documentation-stuff/wiki/special-cheats file.
