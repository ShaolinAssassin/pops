h1. *POPStarter version*


______________________________________________________________________________________________________________

A tip to know which version of POPStarter you are using...

Open POPSTARTER.ELF file with a hex editor and look at offset $59 (blue) on pic below : it's the Build date.
In red, starting at offset $5E is the Build ID. 

!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/1963149811-ELF_ID.JPG(title)!
