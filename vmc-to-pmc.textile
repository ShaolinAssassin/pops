h1. [VMC] *Use your VMC saves on a PS1 retail console*


______________________________________________________________________________________________________________
_[Original french tutorial by Subaru-San]_ "@metagames":http://www.metagames-eu.com/forums/les-tutoriels-playstation-2/convertir-ses-saves-ps1-pour-pops-et-de-pops-pour-mc-ps1-134666.html 

h2.  _Software Requirements :_ 

* "uLaunchELF (any version)":https://bitbucket.org/ShaolinAssassin/popstarter-documentation-stuff/wiki/related-stuff
* "MemcardRex 1.8":https://bitbucket.org/ShaolinAssassin/popstarter-documentation-stuff/wiki/related-stuff

______________________________________________________________________________________________________________

1. [PS2] Copy/paste (uLE) your VMC from POPS directory to USB, then to your PC. 

2. [PC] Launch MemcardRex, click "Open", then "All files", then browse to your VMC. You will see your VMC content.

!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/1060162485-04_bmp10.jpg(title)!

!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/2290081780-14_bmp10.jpg(title)!

3. [PC] Select the save you want to export, click "Export", then choose "RAW single save (B???????????*)", then save. 

!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/126152954-15_bmp10.jpg(title)!

!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/2142941461-16_bmp10.jpg(title)!

!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/3851751281-17_bmp10.jpg(title)!

Your save is now extracted from the VMC.

4. [PS2] Time to import it to your PS1 MC. Use uLE to copy/paste it from USB to PS1 MC.
