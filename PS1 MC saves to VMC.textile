h1. [VMC] *Use your PS1 MC saves with POPStarter*


______________________________________________________________________________________________________________
_[Original french tutorial by Subaru-San]_ "@metagames":http://www.metagames-eu.com/forums/les-tutoriels-playstation-2/convertir-ses-saves-ps1-pour-pops-et-de-pops-pour-mc-ps1-134666.html 

h2.  _Software Requirements :_ 

* uLaunchELF (any version)
* MemcardRex 1.8

______________________________________________________________________________________________________________

1. Gather all the necessary files :
* Copy/paste - with uLE - all your PS1 saves from your PS1 MC to USB, then to your PC;
* Copy/paste - with uLE - the VMC of your game (SLOT1.VMC, in POPS directory) to USB, then to your PC.

2. Launch MemcardRex, click "Open", then "All files", then browse to your VMC.

!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/1060162485-04_bmp10.jpg(title)!

3. Import PS1 saves into the VMC : select the first Free slot, then "Import Save", then browse to your PS1 MC save, select it and you're done. Your PS1 MC save is now in your VMC.

!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/3387794816-05_bmp10.jpg(title)!
!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/1817022192-06_bmp10.jpg(title)!
!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/1144105031-07_bmp10.jpg(title)!

4. If your PS1 game saves different kind of content (game date, settings data, replay data like in this _Gran Turismo_ save), you can also import them by repeating the operation. 

!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/2785125941-10_bmp10.jpg(title)!

5. Time to save your work.

!https://bitbucket.org/repo/Mpy7Eg/images/3460739571-08_bmp10.jpg(title)!

6. Done. Copy/paste your VMC into your POPS directory and enjoy your old PS1 saves with POPS.
